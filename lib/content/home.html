A downtown Springfield pub with Irish roots and a lot of stories to tell.

There are places built to impress people.

Finnegan's Wake was built for people to gather.

For years, folks have come through these doors looking for all kinds of things. A cold beer after work. A place to watch the game. A burger and a conversation. A celebration. A distraction. A familiar face behind the bar. Sometimes, they don't even know what they're looking for until they find it.

That's the thing about neighborhood bars. The good ones become part of people's lives.

They host first dates and retirement parties. They become the meeting spot before concerts and the place you end up afterward. They hold reunions that weren't planned and friendships that were. They witness heartbreak, job promotions, birthday toasts, and the kind of laughter that makes you forget to look at your phone.

Finnegan's Wake has been part of downtown Springfield long enough to have seen all of it.

And we're grateful for every single person who's pulled up a stool.

Open late · 7 days a week

The Kind of Place You Can Be Yourself

We won't pretend to be the fanciest place in town.

The floors creak a little. The brick walls have earned every scratch and scuff. The neon signs weren't chosen by an interior designer. The pool table has seen its fair share of arguments over whether that shot counted.

We like it that way.

Because somewhere along the line, this place stopped being just a bar.

It became familiar. It became dependable. It became the place where people know your name, or at least remember what you're drinking.

You don't have to dress up. You don't have to know anybody. You don't have to be a regular.

Just walk in. We'll make room.

A burger that hits the spot.

Simple things done well. Sometimes that's enough.

About us

Downtown Springfield Has Changed.
We're Still Here.

A lot has changed downtown over the years.

Businesses have come and gone. Buildings have changed hands. New places have opened their doors while others quietly closed them.

Through it all, Finnegan's Wake has remained what it's always been: a place where people can gather without expectation.

A place where construction workers sit next to attorneys.

Where college graduates celebrate their first jobs, and retirees tell stories from careers long finished.

Where old friends pick up conversations right where they left off.

Where newcomers become regulars.

Where everybody belongs a little bit.

We don't take ourselves too seriously. But we take care of our people. Always have.

Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Finnegan's Wake 21+?+
Yes. Finnegan's Wake is a 21+ establishment, so please bring a valid ID with you.
Do you serve food?+
We do. From burgers and appetizers to pub favorites and late-night bites, we've got plenty to pair with your drink.
Do you take reservations?+
We don't take reservations. Just come on in and we'll do our best to find you a spot.
Do you have games?+
Absolutely. We have a pool table, steel-tip darts, foosball, giant Connect 4, skeeball, and even the only Bubble Hockey table in town.
Where do I park?+
Downtown parking is easier than people think. Street parking is available, and free parking garages nearby include Park Central Garage, Heer's Garage, and the College Station Car Park.
Is Finnegan's a sports bar?+
We sure are. We'll happily throw the game on, especially if it's the Chiefs or the Blues.
