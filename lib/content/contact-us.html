Quick answers

Before You Ask…

Do you take reservations?+

For smaller groups, we generally operate on a first-come, first-served basis. If you're planning something larger, give us a call, and we'll see what we can do.

Are you opening early for the game?+

Sometimes, yes. Especially for major sporting events. Check Facebook or give us a call for the latest information.

Can I host a private event?+

Reach out and tell us what you're planning. We'll let you know what options are available.

Do you have parking?+

Downtown Springfield offers nearby street parking and public lots within walking distance of the pub.

Do I need a trivia team?+

Not at all. Bring a team, join a team, or make some new friends while you're here.
